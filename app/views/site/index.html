What is a 'kit'?

A kit is a set of items which comprise a player's standard uniform attire.

Soccer kits required by soccer clubs typically include one or more jerseys, shorts, socks, and warmups.

With Kit-trader, you can offer or search for locally-owned kits with as little as one item (one jersey) or an entire club's required items. Every team starts with a kit.
